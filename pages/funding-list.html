Funding Sources

Get Funded for Good

UK-Based
  • Bethnal Green Ventures Offers investment in early stage ventures and runs an accelerator programme twice a year to help tech for good ventures launch and scale their businesses.
  • Bridges Ventures Social Entrepreneurs Fund Providing sustainable and impact investments since 2002.
  • Good Finance provides information for charities / social-enterprises.
  • Green Angel Syndicate invests in start-ups tackling climate change.
  • SIS (Social Investment Scotland), including SIS Ventures. Investment and loans for Scottish based companies.
  • Nesta impact investment UK government agency for funding innovation to tackle the major challenges faced by older people, children and communities in the UK.
  • Big Issue invest Can provide loans of between £20,000 and £3 million to social enterprises and charities in the UK.
  • RBS Social and Community Capital
  • Social and Sustainable Capital (SASC) Investment funds. Has limited ability to support for-profit companies.
  • Can Invest A resource for investors seeking to invest and UK-based charities/social-enterprises seeking investment.
  • UnLtd Funding of £500 to £15,000 for start-ups and upto £150,000.
  • Mustard Seed Venture capital, investing in high growth impact.
  • Resonance Offers advisory services to help social enterprises and charities raise capital from like-minded investors.
  • Social Finance A nonprofit working in the UK and internationally to combine data, investment, idea incubation, design in partnership with governments, the social sector and investors.
  • Zinc
US-Based
  • Omidyar Capital invest in social change.
  • AngelList Recruit top talent (developers, designers, etc.) by posting job availabilities for free.
International
  • TechStars Europe - Investors List A big list of investors compiled by TechStars a few years back.
  • European Founder Society Establishing a network and providing resources for European purpose-driven tech founders working to impact: Environment, Water & Food, Health, or Energy.
  • Numbers for Good Advisors / consultants for companies and investors.
